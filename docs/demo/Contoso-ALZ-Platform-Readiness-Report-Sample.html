Platform Scale Implication

Contoso's current Azure platform maturity is critically low, with an overall maturity score below 20%. Key foundational controls across security, identity, networking, management, and governance are failing, exposing the business to significant operational, compliance, and reputational risks. The assessment reveals that essential protections—such as data encryption, privileged access management, centralized monitoring, and resilient network architecture—are either absent or not enforced, with most controls either failing or not implemented at all. The primary business risk is the high likelihood of service disruption, data loss, or security breach, as evidenced by the lack of backup coverage, missing DDoS protection, and insufficient RBAC hygiene. Without immediate investment in core landing zone controls, Contoso faces increased probability of regulatory non-compliance, financial loss from outages or breaches, and inability to scale securely. The investment thesis is clear: rapid remediation of these critical gaps will unlock operational continuity, reduce exposure to cyber threats, and establish a foundation for compliant, cost-managed cloud growth.

Top Platform Adoption Risks

Exposure to Data Loss and Breach

ALZ Design Area: Security

Platform impact: Loss of sensitive business data or customer information could result in regulatory penalties, reputational damage, and direct financial loss.

Technical cause: Storage accounts and PaaS services lack required security posture and private endpoint coverage; backup coverage for VMs is 0%.

High Risk of Service Disruption and Lateral Attack

ALZ Design Area: Network Topology and Connectivity

Platform impact: Unprotected network and lack of segmentation increase the likelihood of successful cyberattacks or outages, threatening business continuity.

Technical cause: No hub-spoke topology, no Azure Firewall, and missing DDoS protection on VNets.

Privileged Access Mismanagement

ALZ Design Area: Identity and Access Management

Platform impact: Excessive privileged access increases the risk of accidental or malicious changes, leading to potential outages or data compromise.

Technical cause: RBAC hygiene issues (27 Owner assignments, 0% group-based), and Privileged Identity Management (PIM) not in effect.

Lack of Centralized Security and Monitoring

ALZ Design Area: Management

Platform impact: Inability to detect, respond to, or investigate security incidents in a timely manner, increasing exposure to undetected threats.

Technical cause: Centralized security visibility is lacking; SOC workspace topology is fragmented and Sentinel is not enabled.

Ineffective Cost and Policy Governance

ALZ Design Area: Governance

Platform impact: Potential for uncontrolled cloud spend and non-compliance with internal or external policies, risking budget overruns and audit findings.

Technical cause: Cost forecasting and governance controls are failing or not implemented.
